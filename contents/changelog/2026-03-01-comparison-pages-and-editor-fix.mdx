Comparison pages received a major upgrade with navigation, sharing, and engagement improvements. We also fixed a bug where long posts could break the editor layout.

## What's New

- **Table of Contents on Comparisons** - Each comparison article now has a sticky table of contents on the right side, matching the blog layout. Scroll position is highlighted as you read
- **Share Buttons** - Comparison pages now include social share buttons (LinkedIn, X, Email, Telegram, WhatsApp) alongside the table of contents
- **Reading Progress Indicator** - A progress bar at the top of the page shows how far you have scrolled through a comparison article
- **Inline CTAs** - Added calls-to-action after the feature comparison table in every article, not just at the bottom

## Improvements

- **Table Styling** - Markdown tables now have borders around all cells with rounded corners, replacing the previous minimal separator-only style
- **Product Naming** - Consistently use "LinkedInPreview.com" as the product name across all comparison pages
- **Comparison Index Cards** - Removed the "vs" badge and replaced with full descriptive titles
- **llms.txt** - Added the comparison hub and all individual comparison pages for better LLM discoverability

## Fixes

- **Editor Overflow** - Fixed a bug where entering a very long post would cause the editor to grow beyond its container, hiding the toolbar and action bar. The editor now has a fixed height of 70vh and scrolls internally
