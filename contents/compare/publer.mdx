## What is Publer?

Publer is a social media management platform aimed at agencies, businesses, and marketers managing multiple social channels. Key features include:

- **Multi-platform scheduling**: Publish to LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, Pinterest, and more.
- **Bulk scheduling**: Upload and schedule large volumes of posts at once.
- **Team collaboration**: Assign roles, get approvals, and work with clients.
- **Analytics**: Track performance across all connected accounts.
- **Recycling**: Automatically reshare evergreen content on a set schedule.

Publer is best suited for social media managers and marketing teams who handle multiple accounts across multiple platforms.

## Pricing

Publer's free plan allows up to 3 social media accounts with limited scheduling. Paid plans start at $12/month per user and scale with the number of accounts and team members. AI features require a paid plan.

## When to Use Publer

Publer makes sense if you:

- Manage multiple social media accounts across different platforms.
- Work in an agency or team and need collaboration and approval workflows.
- Need bulk scheduling and content recycling at scale.
- Want a centralized dashboard for all social media activity.

## When to Use LinkedIn Preview

LinkedIn Preview is the better choice when you:

- Focus on LinkedIn specifically and want the most accurate preview possible.
- Need full text formatting (bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, lists) without limitations.
- Want to see your post in a realistic LinkedIn feed simulation before publishing.
- Want free AI-generated post ideas without paying for a subscription.
- Do not want to create an account just to preview how a post will look.

Publer's LinkedIn preview is a secondary feature of a multi-platform tool. LinkedIn Preview is built exclusively for LinkedIn and gets every detail right.

## Conclusion

Publer is a capable choice for social media managers who need to juggle multiple platforms and accounts. For anyone whose primary focus is LinkedIn, LinkedIn Preview provides a faster, freer, and more accurate experience. No account creation, no monthly fees, and a preview that actually looks like LinkedIn.
