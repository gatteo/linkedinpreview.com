## How to Master LinkedIn Search Filters

To access advanced search, use the search bar at the top of your LinkedIn homepage.

1. **Enter a keyword** (e.g., "SaaS Sales").
2. Click **"People"** or **"Posts."**
3. Click **"All filters"** to open the advanced search panel.

### The Most Powerful Filters for B2B Leads

- **Connections**: Filter by 2nd-degree connections to find people you can get introduced to easily.
- **Industry**: Target specific sectors that most need your service.
- **Past Company**: Great for finding alumni or people with shared professional experiences.
- **Company Headcount**: Identify companies that match your ideal size (e.g., 50-200 employees for mid-market focus).

## The Strategic Networking Workflow

Finding leads is only half the battle. How you engage is what converts them.

### 1. The "Soft" Touch

Once you have your list, don't send a connection request immediately.

- Follow their profile first.
- Engage with their posts by leaving a thoughtful, value-driven comment.
- _Then_, send a connection request referencing their recent content or insight.

### 2. Personalize, Personalize, Personalize

As we've covered in our guide to [LinkedIn connection requests](/blog/linkedin-connection-request-messages), generic requests are ignored. Reference a shared interest, mutual connection, or a specific problem they are vocal about.

### 3. Maintain Your Pipeline

Use a simple Notion or CRM system to track the prospects you identify via LinkedIn Search. Don't let your outreach lose momentum - track your connection requests and follow-ups carefully.
