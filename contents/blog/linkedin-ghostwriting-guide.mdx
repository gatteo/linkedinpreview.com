## What Does a LinkedIn Ghostwriter Actually Do?

A LinkedIn ghostwriter does more than just type words. You act as a content strategist and brand voice editor.

Your daily tasks typically include:

- **Interviewing clients** to extract stories, insights, and industry opinions.
- **Drafting content** that fits the client's specific tone and expertise.
- **Formatting text** for maximum readability on mobile devices.
- **Planning content calendars** to ensure consistent publishing.

You are the behind-the-scenes operator making the executive look like a thought leader. The client gets the reach and engagement, and you get paid for your writing expertise.

## How to Become a LinkedIn Ghostwriter

You do not need a degree in journalism to start ghostwriting. You need an understanding of how [LinkedIn personal branding](/blog/linkedin-personal-branding) works and the ability to write clearly.

### 1. Build Your Own Profile First

Clients will look at your LinkedIn profile before hiring you. If your own posts get zero engagement or are poorly formatted, they will not trust you with their brand. Post consistently, test different hooks, and build an audience. Treat your profile as your portfolio.

### 2. Learn the Art of the Hook

The first two lines of a LinkedIn post determine its success. If readers do not click "see more," the algorithm stops showing the post. Study [LinkedIn hook examples](/blog/linkedin-hook-examples) and practice writing opening lines that create curiosity or challenge a common belief.

### 3. Master Post Formatting

A giant wall of text will fail, no matter how good the advice is. LinkedIn ghostwriting relies heavily on structure. You must use:

- Short, punchy sentences.
- Strategic line breaks to create white space.
- Bullet points to organize complex ideas.

## Essential LinkedIn Ghostwriting Tools

To scale a ghostwriting business, you need a streamlined workflow. You cannot rely on messy Word documents or email threads.

- **For capturing ideas:** Use a voice note transcription tool (like Otter.ai or Oasis) to interview clients and turn their spoken thoughts into raw text.
- **For drafting:** Use Notion or Google Docs to organize your content calendar and share drafts for client approval.
- **For formatting and previewing:** Raw text from Google Docs often loses its formatting when pasted into LinkedIn. Copy your text into our [LinkedIn post formatter](/blog/linkedin-post-formatter) to verify line breaks and ensure the hook fits perfectly before the "see more" cutoff.

## FAQ: LinkedIn Ghostwriting

### How much do LinkedIn ghostwriters charge?

Rates vary widely based on experience. Beginners often charge $500 to $1,000 per month for 2-3 posts a week. Experienced ghostwriters working with high-profile founders can charge $3,000 to $5,000+ per month per client.

### Is ghostwriting allowed on LinkedIn?

Yes. LinkedIn does not penalize accounts for having a team member or agency write their content. However, the content must be authentic to the person posting it.

### How do I find my first client?

Start by engaging with founders or executives who post inconsistently. Leave thoughtful comments on their posts, send a connection request, and offer to rewrite one of their underperforming posts for free to prove your value.
