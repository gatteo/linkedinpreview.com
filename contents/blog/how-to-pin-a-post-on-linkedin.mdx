However, the process of pinning content works differently depending on whether you are managing a **Company Page**, a **Personal Profile**, or a **LinkedIn Group**.

In this guide, we will break down exactly how to pin a post on LinkedIn in 2026, including the relatively new feature that allows you to pin comments to drive better discussions.

## How to Pin a Post on a LinkedIn Company Page

If you are a Page Admin, pinning a post to the top of your Company Page is straightforward. This ensures that anyone visiting your page sees your priority content first.

**Here is how to do it:**

1. Navigate to your **LinkedIn Company Page** (you must be in Admin view).
2. Click on the **Posts** tab.
3. Scroll down to find the published post you want to pin.
4. Click the **three dots (...)** in the upper-right corner of that post.
5. Select **Pin to top** from the dropdown menu.

Once pinned, the post will display a "Pinned" label in the corner and stay at the top of your Page's feed. To unpin it, simply click the three dots again and select **Unpin from top**. You can only have one pinned post active at a time.

## How to "Pin" a Post on a Personal Profile

A common question is: _Can I pin a post on my personal LinkedIn profile?_

The short answer is no, LinkedIn does not have a traditional "pin" button for personal profile feeds. However, they offer something even better: **The Featured Section**.

The Featured section sits prominently near the top of your profile (just below your About section) and acts as a visual highlight reel for your best work.

**How to add a post to your Featured section:**

1. Go to your **LinkedIn Profile**.
2. Scroll down to the **Activity** section and find the post you want to highlight.
3. Click the **three dots (...)** in the top-right corner of the post.
4. Select **Feature on top of profile**.

Alternatively, you can go directly to your Featured section, click the **"+" (plus)** icon, select **Add a post**, and choose from your recent content.

## How to Pin a Comment on a LinkedIn Post

Pinning a comment is a fantastic way to guide the conversation, highlight a great question, or add a link without hurting your post's initial reach (the classic "link in comments" strategy).

You can pin a comment on both your personal posts and your Company Page posts.

**How to pin a comment:**

1. Go to your published post and locate the comment section.
2. Find the comment you want to highlight (it can be your own or someone else's).
3. Click the **three dots (...)** next to the comment.
4. Select **Pin comment**.

The comment will now stay at the very top of the thread, marked with a "Pinned" badge. To maximize your engagement, read our deep dive into [LinkedIn commenting strategy](/blog/linkedin-commenting-strategy).

## How to Pin a Post in a LinkedIn Group

If you manage a LinkedIn Group, pinning a post is a great way to welcome new members or highlight community rules.

**Here is how to pin a group post:**

1. Navigate to the LinkedIn Group you manage.
2. Find the post you want to pin.
3. Click the **three dots (...)** in the upper-right corner.
4. Select **Pin**.

The post will now feature a "Pinned by admin" label. You can easily unpin it later by returning to the same menu.

## Best Practices for Pinned Posts

To get the most out of your pinned content, keep these best practices in mind:

- **Rotate regularly:** Don't leave the same post pinned for months. Update it every few weeks to keep your profile or page feeling fresh.
- **Drive action:** Your pinned post should have a clear goal. Use it to drive newsletter signups, promote a new product, or share a high-converting lead magnet.
- **Use eye-catching visuals:** Pinned posts get the most eyeballs. Make sure they include a high-quality image, video, or document carousel.
- **Format for readability:** Before pinning any text-heavy post, ensure it is easy to read. Use [linkedinpreview.com](https://linkedinpreview.com) to check your line breaks, add bold text, and preview the "See more" hook.
