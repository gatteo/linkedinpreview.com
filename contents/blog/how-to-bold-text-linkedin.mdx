## Best Practices for Using Bold Text

Just because you *can* make text bold doesn't mean you should make *everything* bold.

1.  **Be Selective**: Only bold the most critical parts of your message (your core thesis, a shocking statistic, or a clear call to action).
2.  **Aim for Impact**: If everything is bold, nothing stands out. A good rule of thumb is to have 1-3 bold phrases per post.
3.  **Check Mobile Preview**: Always check how your bold text looks on mobile. What looks fine on a desktop screen might look overwhelming on a phone.

## Troubleshooting: Why Isn't My Bold Text Working?

If your bold text isn't displaying correctly on LinkedIn:

- **Check Your Formatter**: Ensure you are using a reliable, LinkedIn-specific formatter like [linkedinpreview.com](https://linkedinpreview.com/#tool). Some generic text converters don't use Unicode characters that are properly recognized by LinkedIn.
- **Browser Issues**: Very rarely, an outdated browser or certain extensions can interfere with the way Unicode characters render. Refreshing the page usually solves this.
- **LinkedIn Updates**: While rare, LinkedIn sometimes updates how they handle Unicode formatting. A dedicated, active tool (like ours) will always be updated to handle these changes.
